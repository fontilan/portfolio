My projects

I'm still early on my way towards being the best developer in the world, but I'm quite happy with this particular project. The challenge here was one familiar to every beginner web developer - to design a website that is responsive, so that it changes its layout depending on the size of the screen that it's viewed on.

Or in other words - it's supposed to look good on a small screen of a mobile device.

For the content I chose to include interesting book covers of my favourite writer - Franz Kafka - and create a gallery with short descriptions of his works and a short bio.

Since at the time of making it I wasn't thinking of sharing it with the world (except my friends), it is in Polish. I might update it some day (I still want to add a responsive mobile navigation bar) and maybe translate the descriptions into English.
