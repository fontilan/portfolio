Kafka jak z obrazka

My very first website was one dedicated to my cat, but it was bad in all sorts of ways. This is the second website I made.

I wanted to start simple by creating a picture gallery of interesting book covers of my favourite writer - Franz Kafka. Then I added some descriptions of his works and a short bio. I also spent a lot of time trying to get display:grid to work properly, but in the end I think it looks decent.

Challenges here: everything. No, seriously, after only a few weeks of learning almost everything was a challenge for me at this point, especially CSS and grid display in particular.

Utilizes: HTML, CSS.
